Meet the Team

Hand-Picked. Battle-Tested. Dedicated to You.

Boaz Saar

Founder & Head of Method

Leadership Program Design Precision Training

Former Israeli military officer who brought the same strategic precision to fitness. Boaz hand-picked every trainer on this team and designed the multi-trainer rotation model that sets this studio apart. Over a decade running the Upper East Side's most discreet, results-driven fitness operation. Scientific in approach, relentless in execution, and personally invested in every client's transformation.

Heather Culton

Movement & Recovery Specialist

Ballet Yoga Pilates Flexibility

Former professional dancer turned fitness architect. Heather fuses ballet, yoga, and Pilates into sessions that target the small muscle groups most trainers overlook. Her specialty: injury prevention, postural correction, alignment, and the kind of fluid, graceful movement that makes everything else — from lifting to running to daily life — feel effortless. She doesn't just train your body. She teaches it to move the way it was designed to.

Luis Colon

Strength & Conditioning Specialist

Boxing Strength Speed NASM CPT

Born and raised in New York City, son of a professional boxer. Luis carries that ring discipline into every session — sharp, focused, and technically precise. NASM Certified Personal Trainer with deep competitive boxing experience and a track record training NYC's most demanding clientele. He builds raw strength and explosive speed, but what sets him apart is his ability to push you past limits you didn't know you had — safely, strategically, and with zero wasted effort.

Lior Shenberg

Personal Trainer & Self-Defense Instructor

Krav Maga Karate Muay Thai IDF

Martial arts since 13 in Tel Aviv. Karate Black Belt. Former IDF Krav Maga instructor for the Military Police Academy — where he trained detectives and intelligence unit operatives. Spent three years at one of NYC's largest Krav Maga schools before joining Boaz Studios. Lior built a dedicated kids' self-defense curriculum and brings a rare combination: combat-tested technique delivered with patience and precision. He doesn't teach fear. He teaches control.
