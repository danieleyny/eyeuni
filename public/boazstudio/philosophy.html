Our Philosophy

The Art & Science of
Human Movement

A training methodology forged from military discipline, scientific rigor, and two decades of transforming bodies on the Upper East Side.

The Model

Why Four Trainers Are Better Than One

The fitness industry sells you one trainer and hopes for the best. Boaz rejected that model in 2006 — because no single person, however talented, can master every dimension of human performance.

At Boaz, you rotate across four specialists. One session focuses on raw strength. The next on explosive speed. Then flexibility and postural alignment. Then self-defense. Each trainer brings a different lens, a different skill set, a different way of challenging your body.

  • No training plateaus — your body never adapts to one style
  • Four expert perspectives on your movement and progress
  • Programs designed collaboratively, not in isolation
  • Every imbalance spotted, every weakness addressed
The Method

Kinesiology-First. Always.

Every program at Boaz starts with how your body actually moves — not with a cookie-cutter template. We assess your posture, your gait, your range of motion, your injury history. Then we build.

The result is training that respects your body's mechanics while systematically expanding its capabilities. Safe progression. Measurable results. Zero guesswork.

  • Movement assessment before your first real session
  • Programming that adapts as your body evolves
  • Injury prevention built into every routine
  • Backed by biomechanics, not fitness trends
