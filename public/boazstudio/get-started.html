You are not simply purchasing access to a facility. You are embarking on a journey with a coaching team that cares about improving your life.

How It Works

Three Steps to a Stronger You

Simple, transparent, and designed to put you in control from day one.

01

Fitness Consultation

We begin your personal training journey with a one-on-one consultation. This includes a basic workout — challenging, but not complex — designed to evaluate your movement. We also discuss your overall goals and fitness experiences.

02

Follow Up

The Boaz Fitness team meets as a group to discuss your plan of action. You will understand exactly why our program will work for you.

03

Your Decision

You will get to work with all 4 trainers who work around your busy schedule to make fitness part of your life. There are no joining fees or monthly dues.

What to Expect

Common Concerns, Honest Answers

Wherever you're starting from, we've helped someone just like you.

"I've never worked with a trainer before."

Perfect. We specialize in meeting you exactly where you are. Your first session is a conversation and evaluation — not a test. Most of our long-term clients started as first-timers.

"I'm recovering from injury or surgery."

Our kinesiology-first approach was built for this. We've helped clients recover from major surgeries, chronic pain, and debilitating conditions. Safe progression isn't a feature — it's the foundation.

"I don't have time."

We're open 5:30am to 10pm on weekdays and 8am to 6pm on weekends. Sessions are scheduled around your life. Most clients train 2-3 times per week for maximum results with minimum disruption.
