Your Instructor

Meet Lior Shenberg

Lior Shenberg

Personal Trainer & Self-Defense Instructor

Lior's martial arts training began at 13 in Tel Aviv — Karate, Muay Thai, and eventually Krav Maga as a hand-to-hand combat instructor for the Israeli Defense Forces Military Police Academy. He trained detectives and intelligence operatives before bringing his expertise to New York City.

After three years teaching at one of NYC's largest Krav Maga schools, Lior joined Boaz Studios to offer private, precision-focused self-defense training in a refined setting. He also developed a dedicated kids' self-defense curriculum.

  • Karate Black Belt
  • IDF Krav Maga Instructor Certification
  • Wingate Institute Personal Training Certification
  • Former IDF Military Police Academy Instructor
  • 3+ years at top NYC Krav Maga school
